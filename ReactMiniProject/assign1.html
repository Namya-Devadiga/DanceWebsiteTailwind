Introduction

Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest and most popular classical dance forms of India, originating from Tamil Nadu. It combines expressions (Bhava), melody (Raga), rhythm (Tala), and dance (Natya), representing devotion and spirituality through graceful movements.

Bharatanatyam Highlights

Graceful Poses

Bharatanatyam poses reflect the beauty and devotion towards divine forms.

Traditional Costumes

The rich silk sarees and temple jewelry symbolize elegance in Bharatanatyam.

Expressions (Bhava)

The powerful facial expressions express stories from mythology.
